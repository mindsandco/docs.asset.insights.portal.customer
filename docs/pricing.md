# Pricing

Simple and predictable. You pay a one-time base fee to get onboarded and connected, then a recurring subscription sized to how much of your estate you bring into the platform.

## How pricing works

**1. Base fee: onboarding and access**

A one-time fee of **€13.400** that covers standing up your tenant, connecting your first assets, harmonizing your data into the canonical model, and giving your team access to the portal and APIs. This is what gets you live.

**2. Subscription: sized by registered fields**

Your recurring price is based on the number of **fields registered** in the platform. A field is a group of assets you manage together, for example a solar site, a wind park, or a portfolio cluster. The more fields you bring into the platform, the more of your estate it covers, so pricing scales in clear bands rather than per individual asset or per user.

## What counts as a field?

A field is a group of assets you manage together, such as a solar site, a wind park, or a portfolio cluster. You register the field once and the platform tracks every asset within it. You only pay for the fields you actually bring into the platform.

## Hosting

Prefer not to run the platform yourself? We can host it for you as a fully managed service, so your team gets the value without operating any infrastructure. Hosting is priced by the size and demands of your deployment, from **€270 to €13.400 per month**. Tell us about your estate and we will quote the right fit.

## Support agreements

Need guaranteed response times, priority handling, or a dedicated point of contact? We offer support agreements tailored to how critical the platform is to your operation. **Talk with us** and we will size a support plan around your needs.

## Not sure which tier fits?

Most customers start with a focused pilot, see the value on their own data, then grow into the tier that matches their estate. We will help you map your assets and fields to the right band before you commit.
